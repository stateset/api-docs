## What is StateSet?

StateSet is an AI-powered software as a service (SaaS) company focused on DTC commerce space. Our mission is to revolutionize commerce operations with a cutting-edge platform that drives efficiency, collaboration, and scalability, enabling merchants and their partners to achieve the performance of large brands with a fraction of the resources. Our vision is to become the leading technology provider in the commerce industry. 


## Why StateSet?

StateSet has developed proprietary software for automating operations, reverse logistics and other post-purchase workflows leveraging temporal framework, Hasura graphql engine and react server components (RSC) framework. StateSet has also developed a RAG AI platform for developing autonomous agents powering thousands of customer requests per month regarding WISMO, returns, and other product questions. Our comittment to delivering a next-generation technology platform is fueled by our passion for helping merchants and their partners achieve their goals.
