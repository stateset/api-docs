# ReSponse AI

### What is the ReSponse app?

ReSponse App, is specifically designed for Shopify merchants to resolve customer service tickets.

### How it works?

1. Interprets the question / comment / request from the customer
2. Generates the correct response based on your company knowledge base, FAQs & APIs
3. Automatically writes back to the customer with the correct answer

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/Frame 1157 (1) (1).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>response.cx</p></figcaption></figure>

### What's it made of?

ReSponse App leverages the cutting-edge capabilities of OpenAI's GPT-4 model, a groundbreaking large language model known for its ability to understand and generate human-like text. By incorporating GPT-4 into its core, ReSponse can provide users with an intuitive interface for updating and finetuning the AI assistant with their own company data, knowledge and FAQ. Thus, empowering the business to achieve greater efficiency and productivity when it comes to customer service automation and commerce operations.



<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/ReSponse.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Customers can "Add" and "Update" data from their company repository to ReSponse. ReSponse will use this data when "Answering" back to customers automatically.

### **ReSponse App Points**

Points can be earned by users which will earn a % discount (capped at 30%) per month.

| Action | Points Earned |
| ------ | ------------- |
| Add    | +15           |
| Update | +10           |
| Answer | +5            |

Every 1000 points is 10% off of the monthly bill with a maximum of 3000 points per month.

### **ReSponse and Gorgias**

ReSponse automatically integrates with Gorgias tickets, alleviating the manual effort required for customer service representatives. Gorgias, a popular helpdesk platform tailored for e-commerce stores, offers seamless multichannel customer support from a single interface. By employing Stateset ReSponse, businesses can effortlessly improve response times and overall customer satisfaction.

To start using Stateset ReSponse, users simply need to visit https://chat.response.cx and follow the straightforward installation steps.
