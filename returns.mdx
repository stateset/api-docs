Returns describes the general data about the goods or services being returned by your customers. For example, you might have multiple line items on an order, each would be a separate Return Line Item. The Return is corresponding to the metadata regarding Returned Items.


## Attributes

  <ResponseField name="id" type="Integer" required>Unique identifier for the entry in the table</ResponseField>
  <ResponseField name="created_date" type="Date/Time" required>Date and time when the entry was created</ResponseField>
  <ResponseField name="amount" type="Decimal" required>The monetary amount associated with the entry</ResponseField>
  <ResponseField name="action_needed" type="Boolean" required>Indicates whether any action is required for the entry</ResponseField>
  <ResponseField name="condition" type="String" required>Describes the condition of the item or situation</ResponseField>
  <ResponseField name="customerEmail" type="String" required>Email address of the customer associated with the entry</ResponseField>
  <ResponseField name="customer_id" type="Integer" required>Unique identifier for the customer associated with the entry</ResponseField>
  <ResponseField name="description" type="String" required>Brief description or additional details about the entry</ResponseField>
  <ResponseField name="enteredBy" type="String" required>Name or identifier of the person who entered the entry</ResponseField>
  <ResponseField name="flat_rate_shipping" type="Boolean" required>Indicates whether the shipping rate is a flat rate</ResponseField>
  <ResponseField name="order_date" type="Date/Time" required>Date and time when the order was placed</ResponseField>
  <ResponseField name="order_id" type="Integer" required>Unique identifier for the order associated with the entry</ResponseField>
  <ResponseField name="reason_category" type="String" required>Category or reason associated with the entry</ResponseField>
  <ResponseField name="reported_condition" type="String" required>Condition as reported by the customer or user</ResponseField>
  <ResponseField name="requested_date" type="Date/Time" required>Date and time when a specific request was made for the entry</ResponseField>
  <ResponseField name="rma" type="String" required>Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number for the entry</ResponseField>
  <ResponseField name="serial_number" type="String" required>Unique serial number associated with the item in the entry</ResponseField>
  <ResponseField name="shipped_date" type="Date/Time" required>Date and time when the item was shipped or dispatched</ResponseField>
  <ResponseField name="status" type="String" required>Current status or state of the entry</ResponseField>
  <ResponseField name="tax_refunded" type="Decimal" required>The amount of tax refunded for the entry</ResponseField>
  <ResponseField name="total_refunded" type="Decimal" required>The total amount refunded for the entry</ResponseField>
  <ResponseField name="tracking_number" type="String" required>Tracking number associated with the shipment for the entry</ResponseField>
